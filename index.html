Where I Come From

I come from the Balkans, where witchcraft wasn't pretty jars or trending sounds.

It was survival.

It was what women used when they had nothing else.

How I Learned

My craft was taught the Balkan way — through women who whispered, not women who posted.

I still speak in the language of symbols: cards, coffee grounds, smoke, intuition.

And yes… I'm still learning how to explain everything in English, but the magic translates.

What I Offer

People come to me for many things, but my specialties are:
